Our Vision

To create an ecosystem at NIT Patna where every student has the resources to turn their craziest ideas into functional prototypes. We envision a campus buzzing with automation and innovation.

Our Mission

We participate in national events like Robocon and IIT Techfests. We also conduct regular workshops on Arduino, IoT, and 3D printing to upskill freshers and sophomores.

What We Do

Mechanical Design

CAD modelling (SolidWorks), 3D Printing, Fabrication, and chassis design for autonomous bots.

Electronics & IoT

PCB Design, Microcontrollers (Arduino/ESP32), Sensors, Motor Drivers, and embedded systems.

AI & Programming

Computer Vision, ROS (Robot Operating System), Path planning algorithms, and ML integration.
